Vice-President

As Vice-President of RPI-IEEE I am mainly tasked with assisting the President of RPI-IEEE. Those tasks involve serving as ex officio Vice-Chair regarding IEEE reporting, overseeing RPI-IEEE activity, and serving as an official representative of the organization. I am a sophomore Electrical & Computer Systems Engineering dual major and I expect to graduate in 2023. Outside of RPI-IEEE, I am the EDE Project Manager for Rensselaer Motorsport and I also enjoy sports, the outdoors, playing guitar, video games, and everything related to tech and computers.
